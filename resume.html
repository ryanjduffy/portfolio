About

I have worked with web technology for nearly 25 years with extensive knowledge and experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have used and contributed to several front-end UI frameworks and am confortable working within or beyond those tools. I am quick to adapt to new environments and enjoy the challenge of learning new languages.

I have worked both in large enterprise systems spanning mainframes through the web with complex service-based architectures, serverless single-page web applications, and applications designed for consumer electronics. I recognize that there is no one tool that solves every problem and always look to balance the needs of the business, the capabilities of the team, and the suitability of the technology.

I am a kind and loyal leader that excels at strategic planning and aligning people and work to achieve a vision. For the last 2.5 years, I've managed a team of 8 engineers building a UI framework supporting LG's Smart TV platform. We built a team of high performing and collaborative engineers that consistently shipped quality software on schedule. I am very proud of the product we built and culture we fostered to help each other and our customers create great experiences for LG's products. I've enjoyed the challenges of aligning people with the work, mentoring and coaching individuals to reach their potential, and collaborating with technical, product, and design leads to deliver software.
